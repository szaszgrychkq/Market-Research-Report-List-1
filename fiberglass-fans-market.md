<p><strong>The Fiberglass Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Drive</li><li>Direct Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass fans market is primarily divided into two types: belt drive and direct drive. Belt drive fans utilize a system of belts and pulleys to transfer power from the motor to the fan blades, offering flexibility in speed adjustments and efficient airflow management. In contrast, direct drive fans connect the motor directly to the fan blades, resulting in a more compact design with reduced maintenance needs. Both types are favored for their durability, lightweight properties, and resistance to corrosion in various industrial applications.</p></p>
