<p><p>The under-sink filter system market encompasses various technologies for water purification, including reverse osmosis, carbon filtration, ultraviolet (UV) purification, and ultrafiltration. Reverse osmosis systems effectively remove dissolved solids and contaminants, while carbon filters excel in eliminating chlorine and improving taste. UV systems focus on microbiological purification, inactivating pathogens with ultraviolet light. Ultrafiltration uses a membrane to separate larger particles and bacteria without the need for pressure. Other technologies may include ceramic and ion exchange filters, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
