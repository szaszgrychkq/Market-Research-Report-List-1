<p><p>The infectious disease testing kits market comprises three main types: Molecular Diagnostic Tests, Point-of-Care Testing (POCT), and Immunodiagnostic Tests. Molecular Diagnostic Tests utilize techniques like PCR to detect pathogen genetic material quickly and accurately. POCT offers rapid results at the site of patient care, enhancing immediate decision-making. Immunodiagnostic Tests identify pathogens through antigen-antibody reactions, often employing methods like ELISA. Together, these testing methods support timely diagnosis and management of infectious diseases, improving healthcare outcomes globally.</p></p>
