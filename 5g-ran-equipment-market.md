<p><p>The 5G RAN equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: 5G Non-Standalone (NSA) and 5G Standalone (SA) equipment. </p><p>5G NSA equipment facilitates the integration of 5G networks with existing 4G infrastructure, enhancing performance without a complete overhaul. </p><p>In contrast, 5G SA equipment operates independently of 4G, leveraging advanced features like network slicing for optimized performance and low latency. This distinction is vital for network operators as they transition to fully realized 5G capabilities.</p></p>
