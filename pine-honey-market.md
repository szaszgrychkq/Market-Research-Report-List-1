<p><strong>The Pine Honey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extracted Honey</li><li>Pressed Honey</li><li>Comb Honey</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pine honey market can be categorized into three distinct types: extracted honey, pressed honey, and comb honey. Extracted honey is produced by removing honey from the comb using mechanical means, resulting in a liquid product. Pressed honey involves crushing the honeycomb and extracting the liquid through pressure, often retaining some wax particles. Comb honey, on the other hand, is harvested in its natural form, with honey still contained within the wax cells, offering a unique texture and taste experience.</p></p>
