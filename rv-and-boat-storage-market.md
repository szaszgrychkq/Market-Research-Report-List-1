<p><p>The RV and boat storage market encompasses three primary types: uncovered storage, covered storage, and fully enclosed storage. Uncovered storage offers minimal protection and is typically an open lot for vehicles. Covered storage provides a roof structure, shielding vehicles from direct sunlight and weather elements, while still leaving sides open. Fully enclosed storage offers the highest level of protection, featuring secure garage-like spaces that safeguard vehicles from external conditions, theft, and vandalism, making it ideal for long-term storage.</p></p>
