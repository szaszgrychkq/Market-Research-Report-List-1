<p><ul><li>CO2 Glass Laser Tube</li><li>CO2 Radio Frequency Laser Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 laser tube market comprises two primary types: CO2 glass laser tubes and CO2 radio frequency (RF) laser tubes. CO2 glass laser tubes utilize a sealed glass structure that contains gas mixtures, offering efficient laser output for various applications, including engraving and cutting. In contrast, CO2 RF laser tubes rely on radio frequencies to excite the gas within, providing higher beam quality and longer operational lifespans, making them ideal for industrial uses. Both types serve distinct needs in laser technology.</p></p>
