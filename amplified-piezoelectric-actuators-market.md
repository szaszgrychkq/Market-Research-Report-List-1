<p><p>Amplified piezoelectric actuators are specialized devices that convert electrical energy into precise mechanical motion using piezoelectric materials. The market for these actuators features two main types: stack actuators and stripe actuators. Stack actuators consist of several piezoelectric layers stacked together, providing high force and limited displacement, making them ideal for applications requiring precision. In contrast, stripe actuators utilize a single layer in a more elongated form, offering larger displacements with lower force, suitable for applications like adaptive optics and precision positioning.</p></p>
