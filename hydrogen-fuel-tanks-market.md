<p><p>The hydrogen fuel tank market encompasses five types based on construction materials and pressure capabilities. Type I tanks are rigid metal cylinders, offering high strength but being heavy. Type II tanks are metal with a composite overwrap, reducing weight. Type III tanks feature a composite shell over a metal liner, providing better weight efficiency. Type IV tanks, made entirely from composite materials with no metal, are the lightest and safest. Type V is an emerging category focusing on advanced materials and technologies, enhancing performance.</p></p>
