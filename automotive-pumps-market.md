<p><p>The automotive pumps market is categorized into mechanical and electrical pumps. Mechanical pumps, traditionally driven by the engine, utilize mechanical energy to circulate fluids, commonly used for fuel, oil, and water circulation in vehicles. In contrast, electrical pumps operate independently of the engine, relying on electric motors for fluid movement. This category is gaining traction due to advancements in hybrid and electric vehicles, enhancing fuel efficiency and performance. Both types play crucial roles in managing fluid dynamics within automotive systems.</p></p>
