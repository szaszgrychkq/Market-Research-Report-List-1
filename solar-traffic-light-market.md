<p><p>The solar traffic light market is divided into two main types: fixed and mobile. Fixed solar traffic lights are permanently installed at specific locations, providing consistent traffic management and safety in areas with limited electricity access. In contrast, mobile solar traffic lights are portable solutions that can be easily relocated for temporary traffic control, such as during road construction or events. Both types utilize solar power for energy efficiency and sustainability, making them increasingly popular in urban planning and traffic management.</p></p>
