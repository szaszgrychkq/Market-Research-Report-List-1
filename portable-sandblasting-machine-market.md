<p><p>The Portable Sandblasting Machine market is divided into two main types: dry and wet sandblasting. Dry sandblasting utilizes compressed air to propel abrasive materials, making it ideal for metal surfaces and achieving a rough finish. In contrast, wet sandblasting combines water with abrasives, reducing dust and enhancing surface cleaning, while minimizing material loss. The choice between dry and wet methods caters to different applications, with dry suitable for heavy-duty tasks and wet preferred for delicate surfaces or environments requiring reduced airborne particles.</p></p>
