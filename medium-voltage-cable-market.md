Aluminum cables, being lighter and more cost-effective, are widely used in various industrial settings. Aluminum alloy cables offer a balanced combination of lightweight properties and improved strength, providing an efficient alternative while reducing installation costs. Each type caters to specific needs in power distribution and transmission.</p></p>
