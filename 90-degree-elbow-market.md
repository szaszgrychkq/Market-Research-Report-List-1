<p><p>The 90-degree elbow market is categorized into two main types: short radius elbows and long radius elbows. Short radius elbows have a smaller bend radius, making them ideal for tight spaces and requiring less piping length, often used in compact systems. In contrast, long radius elbows feature a more gradual curve, which helps reduce turbulence and pressure loss, making them suitable for larger, more complex piping networks. Both types play crucial roles in directing flow in various industrial applications.</p></p>
