<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave Sensor</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic wave sensors market primarily includes Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensors. SAW sensors utilize surface waves that travel along the sensor's surface, making them ideal for applications requiring high sensitivity and compact designs, such as in wireless communication and environmental monitoring. In contrast, BAW sensors operate through bulk waves that travel through the material, offering higher frequency performance and robustness, making them suitable for applications in telecommunications and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
