<p><p>The FBG (Fiber Bragg Grating) temperature sensors market is divided into two main types: distributed sensing and fiber optic point sensing. Distributed sensing utilizes a continuous fiber optic cable to measure temperature variations along its length, providing spatially resolved data over large areas. In contrast, fiber optic point sensing focuses on specific locations, utilizing discrete FBG sensors to achieve high precision at targeted points. Both types enable real-time monitoring and are essential in various industries, including energy, construction, and healthcare.</p></p>
