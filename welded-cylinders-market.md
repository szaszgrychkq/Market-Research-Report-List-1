<p><p>The welded cylinders market encompasses two main types: welded hydraulic cylinders and welded pneumatic cylinders. Welded hydraulic cylinders are designed to handle high-pressure applications, utilizing hydraulic fluid for power transmission, and are commonly used in construction and manufacturing. In contrast, welded pneumatic cylinders operate using compressed air, making them suitable for lighter tasks in automation and packaging industries. Both types are characterized by their robust construction and durability, making them essential components in various industrial applications.</p></p>
